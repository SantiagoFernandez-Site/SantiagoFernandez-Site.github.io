Between 2006 and 2024, I worked as an art director in the creative departments of agencies such as Young & Rubicam, Geometry, VML, FCB and Digitas, developing campaigns for brands including Quilmes, Flow, Oreo, Olé, YPF, Movistar, Coca-Cola, Banco Galicia, Arcor, Danone, Revista G7, Musimundo, Chevrolet and Ala.

During those years, my work received recognition at national and international creativity festivals, including Martín Fierro, LIA International Awards, Diente and El Ojo de Iberoamérica.

In 2024, I decided to focus my career on design, deepening my work in visual identity, branding, graphic systems, content production and art direction to develop a broader and more integral perspective.

I currently work at Digitas, a Publicis Groupe agency, where I continue to develop projects from that approach.
