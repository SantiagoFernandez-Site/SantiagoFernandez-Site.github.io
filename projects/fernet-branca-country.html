Fernet Branca Country

An AI experiment inspired by classic Marlboro advertising, Sergio Leone's films and the iconic music of Ennio Morricone.

Imagining what a Fernet Branca commercial might have looked like in the 1960s, I brought together two seemingly distant worlds: spaghetti westerns and Fernet.

The film was created entirely from AI-generated still images, carefully crafted and assembled into a cinematic sequence.
